WHO WE ARE

McGnuts is a leading groundnut processing establishment located in Ogun State, Nigeria. We are concerned about the health of 'groundnut-lovers' and that is why we are big on clean production environment and safe manufacturing process. McGnuts was established on the 5th of February, 2022 with the aim to provide residents of the Redemption City Of God with quality roasted groundnut, but has since then expanded to supplying her environs. We are indeed your go-to groundnut plug.
